Joe's Story

Music

Music is and has always been a huge part of Joe's life. An avid songwriter and multi-instrumentalist, he recieved a Bachelor's of Music Degree at Sonoma State University where his primary instrument was the bassoon. Joe debuted some of his own songs with rock groups at the school.

Hobbies

Aside from music, some of Joe's hobbies include hiking, cooking, playing video games, and learning computer programming. He is a big fan of coffee, tea, wine, and craft beer. He spends much of his free time with his girlfriend, Megan, and their two cats, Smokey and Venus.

Location

Joe currently lives in Bass Lake, California. Though he was born in Los Angeles and lived most of his life in Sonoma County, Joe is quickly finding a place in the communities of the Sierras and the Central Valley, such as the lovely people of Bitwise Industries in Fresno.
